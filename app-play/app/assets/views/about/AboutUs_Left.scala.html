What is ShockTrade?

ShockTrade is a Social Online Game and Trading Simulation designed to allow users to:

  • Test investment strategies
  • Learn how to invest more effectively
  • Or my personal favorite: Compete with riends for the bragging rights of who's actually the best investor

And you can do all of this without risking any of your own capital!

How it works

ShockTrade uses your existing acebook credentials for authentication. So, if you already have a acebook account, all you need to do is Sign up and get started. Once you've signed up just:

  • Join an existing game or create a new one
  • Play with friends or make new ones
  • Hone your existing skills or Learn ones

Competing with Friends

ShockTrade is also a MMO (Massively Multiplayer Online) game with many built-in Social-networking capabilities.

  • Play with your acebook friends
  • Post your triumphs and accompliments to acebook

Research Features

ShockTrade has many built-in securities research utilities, including:

  • Screener — search for securities that meet your specific criteria
  • Explore — search for securities by Sector and Industry
  • Discover — view news and detailed information about specific securities

Are you a Developer?

If you're a Java or Scala developer, download the ShockTrade SDK (coming soon) and develop your own custom trading robots. Trading Robots are software modules that autonomously perform trading of securities, and are capable of joining existing games. Your trading robots can compete with real players and create "street cred" for you.
