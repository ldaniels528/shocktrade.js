    Company Profile for Onyx Service & Solutions Inc (ONYX)

    Onyx Service & Solutions, Inc. provides privately-owned and company-owned automatic teller machines in Onondaga County, New York. The Company's revenues come from the collection of surcharge and inter-exchange revenues.
