Customer Problem

According to CNN Money: "Wall Street welcomed millennials to the world of investing by showing them the scariest financial crisis since the Great Depression" (Egan, 2015).

Millennials, according to a Bankrate.com survey (Tuchman, 2015):
  • Roughly 1 in 4 (26%) own stocks
  • Of those that don’t invest, the reasons are both compelling and frustrating.
  • More than half (53%) said they don’t have the money to invest.
  • One in five (21%) said they don’t know enough about stocks to invest.
According to a Capital One ShareBuilder survey: "93% of millennials say that both distrust of markets and lack of investing knowledge make them less confident about investing," (Palmer, 2014) which represents a large potential consumer market for investment products that allow them to experiment with the market without risking their capital (Sources: 1, 2, 3).

Our Solution

Provides a solution to address Millenials greatest concerns:
  • Not knowing how to invest
  • Fear of losing money while learning
  • Distrust of the markets
Combines the two activities they engage in most:
  • Social Media
  • Video Gaming
ShockTrade combines a trading simulation with social media (Facebook) and video-gaming features, and fosters an environment where valuable investment skills are learned by encouraging light-hearted competition between friends, while eliminating any fear of risking real capital.

Target Market

  • Our target market are consumers between the ages of 18 and 49, which represents 42.8% of the U.S. population (136.4 million consumers)
  • Initially we plan to target Millennials (83.1 million consumers) and Generation Xers
  • These groups are heavy consumers of social media and are much more likely to experiment with learning how to invest (Sources: 1, 2, 3, 4).

Business Model

  • ShockTrade uses a free-to-play model with micro-transactions.
    • Users are able to join and play for free
    • Perks, features that offer players a competitive advantage, are offered for a nominal fee (10 Perks = 1 USD)
    • Purchasing additional units of Shock-coin, the in-game currency, is offered for a nominal fee (50,000 = 1 USD)
  • Auction-based in-game advertisements
    • Animated advertisements
    • Click-based advertisements
  • Subscription-based model for quotes
    • End-of-day quotes
    • Intra-day quotes

Competitive Advantage

ShockTrade is different from most other offerings because it combines elements of Console-gaming and Social Networking with an ease-to-use trading simulation to support beginnings to experts. The applications trading features includes:
  • Buying and Selling all stocks from AMEX, NASDAQ, NYSE and OTC exchanges
  • Margin Accounts
  • Stock Options
